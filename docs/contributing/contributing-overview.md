# Overview

We're building something amazing, and we're glad you're here. Thank you for
investing your time and talent into Storyteller!

New to open source? Check out these guides:

- [How to Contribute to Open Source](https://opensource.guide/how-to-contribute/)
- [Building Welcoming Communities](https://opensource.guide/building-community/)

## Ways to Contribute

All kinds of contributions are valuable to us. Here are the many ways you can
contribute to the project:

- Contribute to [server development](server-development) or
  [mobile apps](mobile-development).
- [Improve documentation](how-to-improve-documentation).
- Answer questions on the [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/KhSvFqcrza)
  support forums.
- Review
  [merge requests](https://gitlab.com/storyteller-platform/storyteller/-/merge_requests/?sort=created_date&state=opened&first_page_size=100)
  from other developers.
- [Report bugs](how-to-report-a-bug) or
  [request features](how-to-request-a-feature).
- Give a "thumbs up" 👍 on issues relevant to you.
- Create tutorials and blog posts.
- Talk about Storyteller on social media.
- Donate on [OpenCollective](https://opencollective.com/storyteller).
- Use [ElfHosted](https://store.elfhosted.com/product/storyteller/) or
  [PikaPods](https://www.pikapods.com/pods?run=storyteller) (20% of revenue
  supports Storyteller).
- ... even just starring or forking the
  [repo](https://gitlab.com/storyteller-platform/storyteller) on GitLab!
